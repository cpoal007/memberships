YT MEMBERSHIPS: HOW TO BECOME A MEMBER FOR ELDNACPLAYS VIA ANDROID OR iOS
1. Open the YouTube App
2. Go to the channel eldnacplays
3. Tap the “Join” button
4. View the membership tiers
5. Select a plan and proceed with Google Play payment
1. Open Safari
2. Go to youtube.com and sign in
3. Navigate to the channel eldnacplays
4. Tap the “Join” button (next to Subscribe)
5. Choose a membership tier
6. Complete the payment via Apple Pay or card

*Note: YouTube app on iOS does not support memberships due to Apple policy. Use Safari instead.*
